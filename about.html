About KRISHNA GLOBAL RECRUITMENT SERVICES

KRISHNA GLOBAL RECRUITMENT SERVICES (KGRS) is a professional manpower consultancy based in Telangana, India. We specialize in connecting skilled professionals with international job opportunities across various industries including construction, hospitality, healthcare, and more.

Our mission is to provide reliable, transparent, and efficient recruitment services that benefit both job seekers and employers. We focus on building long-term relationships and delivering quality talent solutions globally.

Our Vision

To become a leading global recruitment partner known for integrity, trust, and excellence.
